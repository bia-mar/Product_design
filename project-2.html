E-commerce Platform

Product Designer • March 2025 - May 2025 • Lisbon, Portugal

Project Overview

Designed and developed a comprehensive e-commerce platform from the ground up, focusing on creating a seamless shopping experience that drives conversions while maintaining brand authenticity and user trust.

Problem Statement

The client needed a modern e-commerce solution that could handle complex product catalogs while providing an intuitive shopping experience. The main challenges were creating effective product discovery mechanisms and reducing cart abandonment rates.

Goals included:

  • Designing an intuitive product browsing and filtering system
  • Creating a streamlined checkout process
  • Building trust through transparent product information
  • Optimizing for mobile shopping experiences

User Research

Through user interviews, analytics review, and competitive analysis, I identified key friction points in the shopping journey. Users wanted quick access to product details, easy comparison tools, and a checkout process that felt secure yet effortless.

Design Approach

The design emphasizes clear product imagery, comprehensive filtering options, and prominent calls-to-action. The checkout process was reduced to three simple steps with persistent cart visibility and multiple payment options for user convenience.

Impact

The platform achieved a 35% increase in conversion rate, 50% reduction in cart abandonment, and 80% positive user feedback in post-launch surveys. Mobile transactions increased by 120% within the first three months.
