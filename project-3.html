Health & Wellness App

UX/UI Designer • January 2025 - March 2025 • Remote

Introduction

A mobile application designed to help users build healthy habits through gamification, social support, and personalized wellness tracking. The app focuses on making health management engaging and sustainable.

The Problem

Most health apps fail to maintain user engagement beyond the first few weeks. Users found existing solutions either too complex, too rigid, or not motivating enough to sustain long-term behavior change.

Research objectives:

  • Understanding what motivates users to maintain healthy habits
  • Identifying barriers to consistent app usage
  • Exploring effective gamification strategies
  • Balancing guidance with user autonomy

Research Insights

User research with 20 participants revealed that successful habit formation requires flexibility, positive reinforcement, and community support. Users wanted an app that adapted to their lifestyle rather than imposing strict routines.

Design Solution

The app features customizable wellness goals, daily micro-challenges, achievement badges, and a supportive community feed. The interface uses positive language and celebrates small wins to build momentum without creating pressure.

Outcome

Beta testing showed 70% of users remained active after 30 days (compared to 25% industry average), with 85% reporting positive behavior changes. The app received a 4.8-star rating with users praising its motivational approach and flexibility.
